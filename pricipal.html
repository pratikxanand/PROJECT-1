PRINCIPAL'S MESSAGE

SR. DANIYA ARACKAL

We at St. Xavier’s school, Jagdishpur strive to respect the unique individuality of each child, give the child right kind of help. We work with core belief that wisdom can be cultivated gradually amongst all individuals. It is not enough to make children literate and academically intelligent, what is more important is to make them wise so that they are able to use their intelligence judicially. We believe that true wisdom is the ability to listen to your heart and that each child’s self esteem, dignity, physical and emotional well being must be cultivated with the same importance as that given to academic and material success. The educational needs of today are very different from those of the past because of growing competition and with the skills required to face the challenges of future. They can do it easily if we can make the surrounding stimulating and purposeful. >Our students are encouraged to develop sound ethical values. Kindness, respect and politeness are instilled within a safe and nurturing environment. Our aim is to develop students who are aware, socially responsible and creative with good self-management and interactive skills. The school therefore has undertaken a great leap in integration information and communication technology into the class room teaching and learning process. The school always tries its best to maintain the highest quality academic standard and provide the wonderful environment for studies. We are continually very proud of our history, our values, our outstanding facilities and the high standards achieved by our students. Our school motto, “ let your light shine” inspires us to become the light bearers in the world. It sums up the commitment and dedication of all staff to nurture the potential of every child, developing personal excellence in a caring environment.

SR. DANIYA ARACKAL

Principal
